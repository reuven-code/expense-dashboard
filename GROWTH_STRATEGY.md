## 📊 **Retention & Churn Mitigation**

### Churn Prevention Strategy

**Week 1-2: Activation**
- Daily check-ins (WhatsApp)
- Quick win: 5+ appointments booked
- Success metric: Customer sees ROI

**Week 3-4: Engagement**
- Weekly tip emails
- Feature tutorials
- Success story sharing

**Month 2: Scaling**
- 1:1 business review calls
- Upsell opportunities identified
- Referral program activated

**Ongoing: Retention**
- Monthly NPS surveys
- Quarterly business reviews
- Proactive support for at-risk customers

### Churn Risk Scoring

Track these red flags per customer:
```
Health Score Calculation:
- Usage frequency (30%): Logins, appointments created
- Completion rate (30%): Actual booked appointments
- Engagement (20%): Dashboard usage
- No-show rate (20%): Trend over time

Score: 0-100
- Green (70+): Retain & expand
- Yellow (40-70): Support & engage
- Red (<40): Immediate intervention needed
```
